“Calorie warning“

The project was created to remind people of the sugar and oil in fast food. Ice cream is made with a lot of sugar, and french fries and fried chicken use a lot of oil. Too much sugar and oil are bad for the physical body. People who don't often eat fast food sometimes get attracted by buy-one-get-one-free promotions and make a purchase. So I changed the text in the ad to remind people who saw it what they would actually get when they purchased the food in the ad. I used the healing brush to remove the ice cream on the two paper cups, used the lasso to get the "sugar cubes" in the picture, then used the clone tool to copy it into two "sugar cubes", and moved them to the original positions of the two ice creams. Then I used the healing brush to remove the sauce from the fried chicken platter and used the lasso tool to get the dripping oil and placed it on the fried chicken and fries platter. For the text part, I also used the healing brush to erase the original content. I used brushes to paint and cover the parts that couldn't be recognized by the healing brush to make the whole picture clearer. Then I used the text tool and brushes to create my own text. I changed the price to "911" emergency number. Because eating too much sugar can cause serious illness! (tool: photoshop)
